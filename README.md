## Overview: Licensed Hidden Corporations (LhCorp)

**Toric Security Services (TSS)** is the **Privacy Based Corporation License Issuing Authority (LhCorp - Licensed Hidden Corporation)**.

This repository serves as the central register and authority for the **LhCorp. License**, which is granted to anonymous services operating on **Tor** or **I2P** hidden networks that have undergone a thorough audit and approval process. Our mission is to promote trust and reliability within the decentralized, privacy-focused service landscape.

---

## üîë What is an LhCorp. License?

The LhCorp. License is a certification issued by Toric Security Services (TSS) that anonymous service operators may integrate into their service's name and display prominently on their homepage.

### **Purpose of the License:**

* **Trust Establishment:** By displaying an LhCorp. License, a hidden service signifies that it has undergone a vetting process and meets the standards set by the License Issuing Authority.
* **User Assurance:** The license acts as a **visual indicator** to users that the service is trusted and adheres to specific operational guidelines, helping users distinguish reliable services from untrusted or malicious ones.
* **Branding & Integration:** Approved services are authorized to use the LhCorp. designation, often integrated into their service name (e.g., *[Your Service Name] LhCorp.*).

---

## üìù Applying for an LhCorp. License

The primary function of this repository is to manage the application and issuance process for the **Licensed Hidden Corporations of Toric Security Services (LhCorp.)**.

If you operate an anonymous **Tor** or **I2P** hidden service, you are invited to apply for a license.

### **Application Process:**

1.  **Fork and Clone:** Fork this repository and clone it locally.
2.  **Create Application File:** In the designated applications directory, create a new file detailing your service, its operations, and the contact information for the operator(s). *Specific application guidelines will be detailed in the `APPLY.md` file.*
3.  **Submit a Pull Request (PR):** Submit a Pull Request with your application file.
4.  **Review:** The Toric Security Services Authority will review the application. This process includes technical verification and operational due diligence.
5.  **Issuance:** If your application is approved, the PR will be merged, and an official **LhCorp. License** will be issued to your service, authorizing you to integrate and display the license. Your service will then be listed in the official directory.
